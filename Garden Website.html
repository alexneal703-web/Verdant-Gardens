Seasonal Garden Guide

Know exactly what to do in your garden each season in South Australia's Mediterranean climate.

🌸
Spring
Sept – Nov
  • Plant warm-season seedlings
  • Apply slow-release fertiliser
  • Begin regular lawn mowing
  • Prune roses after last frost
  • Set up irrigation timers
  • Divide and replant perennials
  • Mulch garden beds
  • Sow annual flower seeds
☀️
Summer
Dec – Feb
  • Deep water 2–3x per week
  • Mow high to shade roots
  • Harvest vegetables regularly
  • Shade cloth for tender plants
  • Remove spent flowers
  • Check irrigation for blockages
  • Apply wetting agent to lawn
  • Avoid fertilising in heat
🍂
Autumn
Mar – May
  • Plant cool-season vegetables
  • Lawn aeration and top dressing
  • Plant bulbs for spring bloom
  • Reduce watering frequency
  • Compost fallen leaves
  • Plant bare-root roses and trees
  • Apply autumn lawn fertiliser
  • Plant natives — ideal season
❄️
Winter
Jun – Aug
  • Prune deciduous trees
  • Plant citrus and stone fruit
  • Hard prune roses
  • Weed while soil is soft
  • Plan spring garden design
  • Service mowers and tools
  • Protect frost-tender plants
  • Order seeds for spring
